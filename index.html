About

I'm a software engineer and Washington State University Data Analytics graduate (3.62 GPA, minors in Mathematics and Criminal Justice). By day I'm a Quality Engineer at HP, where I build internal web tools and test-automation programs, rebuild data infrastructure, and turn ambiguous business and regulatory requirements into reliable tooling and reporting.

On my own time I build things end to end. I've shipped production websites for real clients — like The Pooch Pit, a dog-grooming booking platform with Stripe deposits, a double-booking-proof availability engine, transactional email, and a no-code admin dashboard the owner runs herself, all deployed on PostgreSQL and Prisma with Cloudflare DNS and SSL. I've also built real-time multiplayer adaptations of tabletop games like Lords of Vegas and Betrayal on authoritative game servers, Discord Activity games, and a full-stack cigar-collection PWA with native iOS and Android builds.

I work across the stack in TypeScript, React, Node, and Next.js, with a data background in Python and R. I like hard problems and shipping things people actually use.
